HOW TO FRY AN EGG

  1. Heat your pan on medium.
  2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of oil or butter
  3. Crack your egg into a small bowl
  4. Gently pour the egg from the bowl into the pan
  5. Cook until the whites are set but the yolk is still runny, about 2-3 minutes
  6. Use a spatula to carefully lift the egg from the pan
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
